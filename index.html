Incremental Game Thesis Project

Every once in a while, a new game genre gains popularity and captivates players all around the globe. Incremental games are one of the latest ones, evolving from the principle of accumulating resources over time.

They are also a focal point of my Thesis project at the IT University of Copenhagen. For the experimental part I have prepared a simple incremental game for all of you to try out.

Every player is valuable, the ones who are new to the genre, the experienced ones, players who absolutely love incremental games and players who love them a little less. In whatever group you are, I suggest playing the game at least to reach a couple of upgrades (you can already stop pushing that mouse button after the second one and discover a new way of collecting drops!).
